About the College

A decade of building futures, one classroom at a time

Since 2014, HMV PU College has stood as a home for ambitious young minds in Nagarbhavi, Bengaluru. What began as a single campus with a clear purpose — to deliver genuinely good Pre-University education — has grown into an institution known for its discipline, warmth, and consistent results.

We believe a PU College is more than a stepping stone to a degree; it is where habits of thought are formed. Our classrooms blend structured teaching with space for questions, debate, and discovery — preparing students not just for board exams, but for the years of study that follow.

Every student who walks through our gates is known by name, tracked in progress, and supported through the academic and personal challenges that come with this stage of life.

Chandra Mohan Sir
Dean, HMV PU College
